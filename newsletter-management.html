ℹ How Automation Works

🤖 Scheduled (set-and-forget)

The cron jobs here run automatically at your configured times. Scraping gathers articles, then the newsletter job generates and sends both CoR Intel Weekly and Safe Freight Mate to all active subscribers — no human involvement needed.

👆 Manual (Operations tab)

The Operations tab lets you preview first then decide. Run Scraping → Generate Preview → review the content → click Send. Useful when you want to check what goes out before it does.

⚡ Trigger Now buttons

These run the full scheduled job immediately — scraping saves articles to the sheet, newsletter generates and sends directly. Same as the automatic run, just fired now.

All times in AEST (Australia/Sydney). Schedule saved to disk — survives restarts.
