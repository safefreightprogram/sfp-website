About the Program

The Safe Freight Program (SFP) is a nationally recognised safety initiative focused on improving safety, professionalism, and operational reliability across the freight and logistics industry.

Our mission is to support drivers, receivers, loaders, and site operators through simple, consistent safety standards. We provide training, digital credentialing, and tools that streamline compliance and reduce on-site risk.

SFP is driven by a vision of shared responsibility across the supply chain — empowering every party to play their role in getting freight delivered safely.
