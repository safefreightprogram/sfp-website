Information We Collect

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Email address (required) - to send you the newsletter

  • Company/organisation and role (optional) - to understand our audience
  • Website usage data - basic analytics to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • • Send you the newsletter content you've subscribed to
  • • Improve our content based on what our audience finds useful
  • • Understand who our readers are (in general, not individually)
  • • Comply with Australian privacy laws and keep proper records

Who We Share Information With

We keep your information private. We only share it with:

  • • Email service providers (like Gmail) to deliver newsletters
  • • Website analytics services to understand how our site is used
  • • Government agencies if required by Australian law

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • • Unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email
  • • Access the information we hold about you
  • • Correct any incorrect information
  • • Request deletion of your information
  • • Make a complaint about how we handle your information

Data Security

We protect your information using secure hosting, encrypted connections, and access controls. We keep your data only as long as needed to provide our service or as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll let you know by email or by posting a notice on our website.
