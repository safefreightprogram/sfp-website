Get Your Safe Freight Passport

The Safe Freight Passport is a digital credential that shows you’ve completed the recognised safety and compliance training for the road freight industry.

Drivers who carry the passport reduce their time on site, improve trust with receivers, and demonstrate professional standards across the industry.

Getting your passport is simple:

  1. Complete the online training (free)
  2. Receive your digital passport with photo and QR code
  3. Carry it on your phone or printed card
