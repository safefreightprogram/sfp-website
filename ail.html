Authorised Inspection Locations (AIL)

The Safe Freight Program accredits eligible facilities as Authorised Inspection Locations (AILs).

AILs play a critical role in verifying driver safety standards and equipment compliance. As part of the network, your site becomes a trusted checkpoint in the national supply chain.

AIL accreditation includes:

  • Digital listing in the national SFP database
  • Tools to verify driver credentials and equipment age
  • Annual licensing and renewal process
